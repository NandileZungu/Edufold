Our global Presence

EDUFORD University is a prestigious international institution with campuses in three iconic cities:
1. London, UK: Our European hub, offering students access to the global financial center.
2. New York City, USA: Our American headquarters, nestled in the heart of Manhattan.
3. Washington, D.C., USA: Our diplomatic hub, connecting students to international relations and policy-making.

Our Mission

At EDUFORD University, we empower students to become global leaders, innovators, and change-makers through:
1. Interdisciplinary education: Integrating theory, practice, and real-world applications.
2. Cultural diversity: Fostering inclusivity, tolerance, and cross-cultural understanding.
3. Research excellence: Advancing knowledge, driving innovation.
